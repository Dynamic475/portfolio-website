What is Memri Connect?

Memri Connect is a smart technology that helps you take control of your data and make smarter choices in life. In short,
helps you weed out the noise and focus on the most important things in shorter time and connect with
the people that are most important to you.

Central Challenge

The main focus was to create a mobile app that smoothly brings together data and relationships.
This app uses both individually and collectively trained smart features to customize your experience.

Cross-functional Collaboration

I worked closely with the founder and the team responsible for building the project from the very beginning.
This ensured that communication between us was clear and effective. Since the project was new and innovative,
good communication was especially important because we were all new to this type of work.

Pattern Identification

I began the design process by studying how people typically interact with similar products already available.
Even though we wanted to introduce new features,
understanding these common patterns helped us create a user-friendly experience that aligns with what people are used to.
We used the same approach for the other three apps.
